Arizona State University

Center for Archaeology and Society

The Center for Archaeology and Society (CAS) at Arizona State University is a hub for collections care, education, public outreach, and research within the School of Human Evolution and Social Change.

This site highlights the public software and infrastructure repositories maintained by the Center for Archaeology and Society GitHub organization.

Visit the CAS website View the GitHub organization

Featured Project

heuristR

heuristR is an R client for Heurist databases with session-based authentication, metadata helpers, safe write workflows, and rollback support for reversible scripted changes.
